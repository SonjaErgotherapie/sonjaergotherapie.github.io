Our Therapy Process: What to Expect

At Sonja Ergotherapie, we are committed to helping you regain independence and improve your daily functioning through personalized ergotherapy. Here’s what you can expect when you start your journey with us:

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment is an opportunity for us to get to know you. We will discuss your medical history, current challenges, and personal goals. A comprehensive assessment will be conducted to evaluate your physical, cognitive, and emotional needs.

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on the initial assessment, we create a tailored treatment plan designed to enhance your independence and well-being. This plan may include:

  • Motor skills training
  • Cognitive therapy
  • Adaptive techniques for daily activities
  • Workplace or home environment modifications
  • Assistive device recommendations

Therapy Sessions

During your therapy sessions, we will work together to implement your personalized plan. Sessions may involve hands-on training, exercises, and real-life simulations to improve your ability to perform daily tasks efficiently and comfortably.

Progress Evaluation & Adjustments

Your progress is continuously monitored to ensure you are meeting your goals. Adjustments to the treatment plan will be made as needed to maximize effectiveness and support your ongoing development.

Discharge & Follow-Up Support

Once your goals have been met, we provide guidance for maintaining your progress at home or work. We also offer follow-up appointments to ensure long-term success and provide additional support if needed.
