Our Specialties: How We Can Help

At Sonja Ergotherapie, we specialize in a range of ergotherapy services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our goal is to enhance independence and improve daily functioning through targeted therapy solutions.

Neurological Rehabilitation

We provide therapy for individuals recovering from conditions such as stroke, multiple sclerosis, and Parkinson’s disease. Our treatments focus on improving mobility, coordination, and cognitive function to enhance daily living.

Pediatric Ergotherapy

We support children with developmental delays, sensory processing disorders, and motor skill difficulties. Our child-friendly approach includes play-based therapy to help improve coordination, attention, and independence.

Workplace Ergonomics & Injury Prevention

We help employees and employers create safer, more efficient workspaces. Our services include ergonomic assessments, posture training, and strategies to prevent repetitive strain injuries.

Mental Health & Well-Being

We assist individuals dealing with anxiety, depression, and stress-related conditions by providing coping strategies, time management techniques, and relaxation exercises to improve overall well-being.

Geriatric Care & Aging Support

We work with older adults to maintain their independence, prevent falls, and improve mobility. Our therapy includes home safety assessments and functional exercises to support aging in place.

Rehabilitation After Injury or Surgery

We help clients recover from orthopedic injuries, surgeries, and fractures by focusing on pain management, strength building, and functional retraining.
